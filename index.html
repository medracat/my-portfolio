I am

a creative person who graduated in a technical field and has learned the importance of functionality as well as the prominence of an aesthetic image. I started off in middle school taking my first steps into the world of graphic design and eventually went into college to complement my acquired knowledge with good-old coding and software engineering principles.

More than ten years later, I've come to understand that design and functionality are very much intertwined and have become dependant on the other: pleasing aesthetics mean nothing without proper functionality, and functionality means nothing if there isn't a pleasant image for the user to feel related to.

To learn more about my academic and technical qualifications or contact me, please see my resume or find me on LinkedIn.
